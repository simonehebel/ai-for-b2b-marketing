# AI Workflow Assessment Template

## Purpose

This template helps B2B marketing teams assess whether an AI-enabled workflow is valuable, feasible, governable and ready for implementation.

The assessment connects:

- business objectives,
- process improvement,
- approved inputs,
- technical feasibility,
- human accountability,
- risk management,
- organizational adoption,
- measurement,
- implementation decisions.

The template should be completed before launching a pilot or scaling an AI-enabled marketing workflow.

## AI Workflow Assessment Checklist

Before approving a pilot, confirm:

- [ ] A real business problem is defined
- [ ] The expected business outcome is clear
- [ ] The workflow scope is documented
- [ ] The AI role is limited
- [ ] Approved inputs are available
- [ ] Input restrictions are understood
- [ ] Technical feasibility is assessed
- [ ] Human review roles are assigned
- [ ] Final accountability remains with a person
- [ ] Risks are documented
- [ ] Mitigation actions have owners
- [ ] Quality criteria are defined
- [ ] Rejection criteria are documented
- [ ] Organizational readiness is assessed
- [ ] Training requirements are clear
- [ ] Baselines and KPIs are available
- [ ] Pilot scope is realistic
- [ ] Success criteria are measurable
- [ ] Stop criteria are defined
- [ ] Required approvals are complete

## Key Principle

An AI-enabled workflow should not be approved because the technology is available.

It should be approved because it:

- solves a real business problem,
- uses reliable and approved inputs,
- keeps human accountability,
- manages risk,
- creates measurable value,
- can be realistically adopted.
